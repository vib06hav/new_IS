# Interview Standardiser

## What this product is

Interview Standardiser is an admissions interview workflow platform that helps teams move from raw application PDF to structured review, interview preparation, live interview execution, and final evaluation.

Its biggest value is not just organizing applications. It is helping interviewers prepare better, ask better questions, stay structured during the interview, and submit stronger final feedback.

## Who it is for

This product is built for admissions teams, especially:

- admissions admins, who upload applications, manage progress, and assign interviewers
- interviewers, who review the application, prepare the interview, conduct it, and submit final feedback

## Highlight features

### AI-generated focus areas

One of the strongest features of the product is that it does not stop at showing the application. It generates focus areas for the interviewer to explore.

These focus areas help answer:

- what stands out in this application
- what deserves deeper probing
- which themes should shape the interview

This makes preparation much more directed and useful than simply reading the PDF.

### AI-generated interview questions

The product also generates question sets based on the applicant’s material and the identified focus areas.

This gives interviewers:

- a starting structure for the conversation
- better opening questions
- more relevant lines of inquiry
- a clearer way to move from application review into interview planning

### Built-in copilot

There is a built-in copilot available inside the workflow. This is one of the key user-facing features.

The copilot helps users:

- understand what they are seeing on a page
- ask questions about the application review
- ask what to do next in the workflow
- navigate more easily between review, prep, interview, and evaluation

For the admissions team, this means the product is not just a static dashboard. It actively supports the user while they work.

### Pre-interview editing and preparation

Before the interview starts, the interviewer gets a preparation workspace where they can review and edit the interview plan.

They can:

- review the generated focus areas
- review the generated question sets
- adjust the interview direction
- add custom questions
- add follow-up prompts
- refine the interview plan before launching the interview

This is important because it keeps the AI-generated output flexible. The interviewer can shape it before the live conversation begins.

### Live interview overlay

During the interview, the product provides a live interview overlay experience instead of leaving the interviewer to manage everything manually.

This allows the interviewer to:

- move through prepared questions
- add follow-up questions in real time
- capture notes while the interview is happening
- mark question outcomes as the conversation progresses

This is one of the clearest standardization features in the product, because it supports structure during the interview itself, not just before or after.

### Note polishing and writing support

The product includes a polish feature that helps improve interviewer-written text.

This can be used to polish:

- question notes
- follow-up notes
- final evaluation summaries

For admissions teams, this means feedback can become clearer, more readable, and more usable without changing the substance of what the interviewer meant.

### Post-interview final editing

After the interview, the interviewer enters a post-interview review flow where they can finalize their assessment.

They can:

- review question-by-question outcomes
- update notes
- refine follow-up observations
- edit and polish the final overall evaluation before submission

This gives the interviewer a proper closing workflow instead of forcing them to submit rough notes immediately after the interview.

## What the overall workflow looks like

### 1. Upload and review

The admissions team uploads applicant PDFs into the system. The product then turns the raw application into a clearer review experience covering the student’s profile, academics, activities, leadership, and essays.

### 2. Generate interview-ready material

The system generates focus areas and question sets so the interviewer is not starting from a blank page.

### 3. Prepare before the interview

The interviewer can edit, reshape, and personalize the generated prep material before the interview begins.

### 4. Run the interview in a structured way

The live overlay supports the interviewer during the conversation with questions, notes, follow-ups, and statuses.

### 5. Complete and polish final feedback

After the interview, the interviewer finalizes notes and the overall evaluation, with support for polishing written feedback before submission.

## Why this matters for an admissions team

The product helps admissions teams in a few especially important ways:

- it reduces the gap between reading an application and knowing how to interview the student
- it helps interviewers prepare with more consistency
- it gives structure during the live interview, not just before it
- it makes post-interview feedback cleaner and easier to compare
- it combines AI support with human control, rather than replacing interviewer judgment

## Short summary

Interview Standardiser is an admissions product built around a few standout capabilities: AI-generated focus areas, AI-generated interview questions, an in-workflow copilot, editable pre-interview planning, a live interview overlay, note polishing, and a structured post-interview final evaluation flow.

In practice, it helps the admissions team turn raw applications into better interviews and stronger final feedback.
